Allergy Child

Oh no... not you again!

Allergy Child is back and she is bored as ever!
She wants to go over to her friend's house but she's always
running into these pesky cats on her way over.
The goal of the game is to get Allergy Child over to
her friend's house safely and without bumping into any cats.

Directions to play the game:

Use the WASD keys to move Allergy Child around the board.

Transport Allergy Child Safely!

W = Up S = Down A = Left D = Right
